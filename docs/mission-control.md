## Proactive Features

Mission Control integrates with the **User Intelligence Memory** to proactively suggest tasks and summaries based on your habits and preferences.

## Voice Messages

Agents can now send voice messages directly to your messaging apps using **ElevenLabs** or **Gemini Native Voice**. Use the `voice_message` tool to trigger audio responses.
